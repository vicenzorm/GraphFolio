# Zenith: The Endless Tower

## App Store (priority)
- Not published yet

## What problem did this solve?
Zenith solves the complexity of scaling action-heavy roguelike systems by using a clean ECS architecture on native iOS.

## What was my role?
- Role title: iOS/Game Developer
- Team size: 5
- Scope owned: ECS design, game systems, SpriteKit optimization, AI behaviors, Game Center integration

## What I specifically built
- Designed and implemented ECS systems for movement, attacks, waves, and AI
- Built procedural wave progression for replayability
- Integrated GameKit leaderboard flow
- Implemented custom joystick/input logic for gameplay precision

## Business impact (most important)
- Architecture: Lower coupling and better long-term maintainability
- Scalability: Dynamic wave system with reduced manual balancing cost
- Engagement: Competitive loop with high-score and leaderboard mechanics

## How I delivered
- Key decisions: ECS over inheritance-heavy game architecture
- Trade-offs: Higher initial setup complexity for better extensibility
- Delivery model: Clear separation between game scene logic and SwiftUI overlays
