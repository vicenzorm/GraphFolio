# GraphFolio

## App Store (priority)
- Not applicable

## What problem did this solve?
Traditional portfolios hide relationships between projects and skills. GraphFolio makes those connections explorable in one visual map.

## What was my role?
- Role title: Creator & Full-stack Developer
- Team size: 1
- Scope owned: Content model, graph parser, frontend interaction, UX flow

## What I specifically built
- Implemented markdown-to-graph pipeline with wikilink edges
- Built interactive D3 graph with hover, search, and detail buffers
- Added guided navigation cues and clearer node affordances
- Designed content conventions for low-friction updates

## Business impact 
- Discoverability: Faster navigation through skills and projects
- Maintainability: New content can be shipped by editing markdown only
- Storytelling: Clear visual narrative of technical depth and domains

## How I delivered
- Key decisions: Astro static architecture + D3 + markdown-first content
- Trade-offs: Prioritized clarity and low maintenance over heavy visual complexity
- Delivery model: Iterative UX improvements focused on readability
