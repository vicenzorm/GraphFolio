# CoreData

## Why I use it
CoreData provides stable local persistence for apps that need reliability, offline behavior, and structured data evolution.

## Proven outcomes
- Stable storage for medium-to-large iOS data models
- Better offline experience and data consistency
- Easier long-term maintenance in established projects
