## Why I use it
Typst helps me produce technical documents quickly with better readability and a simpler syntax than traditional LaTeX workflows.

## Proven outcomes
- Faster iteration in reports and technical writeups
- Cleaner source files with lower formatting overhead
- More maintainable templates for recurring documents
